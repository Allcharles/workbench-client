Basic Details

Harvesting is the name we use for the process of adding collections of audio to the bioacoustic workbench. It is a process that is optimised for mass injestion of data (e.g. 3TB, or 4000 files at a time). This process performs several actions such as:

  • Converting audio files to formats compatible with our website
  • Basic integrity checks on the data harvested
  • Gathering metadata about the audio (format, channels, bitrate, sample rate, duration, etc.)
  • Assigning audio to sites and projects
